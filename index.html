Daily Equipment Pre-Operational Check

Forklift Checklist

Tires: condition and pressure

Forks: condition and pins

Mast/Carriage/Backrest: damage or cracks

Hydraulic hoses/cylinders: leaks or damage

Overhead guard: damage

Fluid levels: oil, hydraulic, coolant, fuel/battery

Seat belt: condition and functioning

Horn, lights, backup alarm: working

Brakes and steering: functioning

No visible leaks under machine

Front-End Loader Checklist

Tires/Tracks: condition, inflation, damage

Bucket/Teeth/Edges: wear, damage, secure

Articulation joint/frame lock: condition

Mirrors, lights, horn, backup alarm: working

Seat belt and cab (ROPS): secure, no damage

Fluid levels: engine oil, hydraulic, coolant, fuel

Hydraulic hoses/cylinders: leaks or damage

Brakes and steering: functioning

No leaks under machine
