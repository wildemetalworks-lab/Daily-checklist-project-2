Daily Equipment Check

Equipment not safe – report defects immediately!

Forklift Checklist

1. Tires: condition and pressure
2. Forks: condition and load rating
3. Mast and chains: wear and lubrication
4. Brakes and parking brake
5. Steering and controls
6. Lights, horn, and backup alarm
7. Fluid levels (oil, coolant, hydraulic)
8. Seatbelt and safety guards

Front-End Loader Checklist

1. Tires / Tracks: condition, pressure, wear
2. Bucket / Blade: condition and teeth
3. Hydraulics: leaks, hoses, cylinders
4. Brakes and steering
5. Lights, horn, backup alarm
6. Fluid levels (engine, hydraulic, transmission)
7. Seatbelt and ROPS
8. Gauges and warning lights
